Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Primed ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Primed mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Quiz answers (age, lifestyle habits, sleep, diet, activity level, stress, sunlight exposure)
  • Apple ID authentication data (via Sign in with Apple)
  • App usage data (habit completions, streak counts, score history)
  • Device information (device type, operating system version)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Calculate and display your personalized testosterone optimization score
  • Generate your personalized daily habit plan
  • Track your progress over time and sync it across devices
  • Send you push notifications related to your daily habits (with your permission)
  • Improve the app and develop new features

Sign in with Apple

We use Sign in with Apple for authentication. When you sign in, Apple may share your name and email address with us, or provide a private relay email at your discretion. We use this only to create and maintain your account. We do not sell or share this information with third parties.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a cloud database provider. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Subscriptions and Payments

All subscription payments are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information. Subscription management is handled through your Apple ID account settings.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — subscription management
  • Supabase — data storage and authentication
  • Apple — authentication and payment processing

Each of these services has their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

Push Notifications

With your permission, we send local push notifications to remind you to complete your daily habits. You can disable these at any time in your device's Settings or within the Primed app under Settings → Notifications.

Children's Privacy

Primed is available to users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@primedapp.co
